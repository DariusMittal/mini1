Topic-Pollution

What is Pollution?

When some harmful substances are released into our environment ; the harm we get is called as pollution.
It is harming our earth in various ways.

Types of Pollution:-

Pollution is of many types but the main types are four which are Air Pollution,Water Pollution,Land Pollution,Noise Pollution.

Cautions of Air Pollution

Air Pollution is caused because of factories that releases a lot of smoke and harmful gases , smoke released by vehicles and cutting down of trees.

Cautions of Water Pollution

Water Pollution is caused because of factories that releases a lot of harmful chemicals into water bodies.

Cautions of Land Pollution

Land Pollution is caused because of dumping of non-biodegradable wastes etc.

Cautions of Noise Pollution

Noise Pollution is caused because of loud speakers,horns of vehicles and loud drums.

How do we can reduce Pollution?

We can reduce Pollution by planting trees,by applying 3Rs :- reuse,reduce,recycle etc.
